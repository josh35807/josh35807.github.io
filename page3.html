How Baller Nation Got Stated

Baller Nation took its first steps here in Eugene and Springfield. Former Olympian and University of Oregon Track Coach Bill Bowerman created a waffle-iron fashioned rubber-sole running shoe, and teamed up with middle-distance UO runner Phil Knight to refine and market this new running shoe.
